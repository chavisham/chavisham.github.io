Triathlon

The sport of triathlon contains the following three sports in this order:

  1. Swimming
  2. Cycling
  3. Running

Although, commonly referred to as 'the fourth sport' are the transitions between each of them, as a triathlon is a continuous race.

  1. Swimming
    • Transition 1 (Swim to Bike)
  2. Cycling
    • Transition 2 (Bike to Run)
  3. Running

There are a number of different race distances for triathlon which athletes are able to compete over, although the 'Standard', or 'Olympic' distance is the primary distance for elite racing.

Race Swim (m) Bike (km) Run (km)
Super-Sprint 400 10 2.5
Sprint 750 20 5
Standard (Olympic) 1500 40 10
Middle distance (Half Ironman) 1900 90 21.1
Long distance (Ironman) 3800 180 42.2
